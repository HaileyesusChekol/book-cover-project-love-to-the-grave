Love to the Grave

It is an Amharic novel by Haddis Alemayehu published in 1968. It is one of the leading known books in Ethiopia and is considered a classic of Ethiopian literature. The novel picked up ubiquity to a great extent due to its far reaching dispersal on Ethiopian radio. It was included on the well known radio program "Kemetsahifit Alem" (The world of Books) by have Wegayehu Nigatu. The creator himself afterward lauded Wegayehu for quote "giving life to the characters within the story" with his portrayal of the novel..

About The Book

The novel is one of the foremost celebrated in Ethiopia. It is especially well known among those people who lived through the 1970s and 80's. during which it was once described over the radio. A music video titled "Mar eske Twauf" by the grant winning Ethiopian music star Teddy Afro based on this novel was released on YouTube.

The books centres on a romance between the beautiful Seble, girl of a nobleman called Meshesha. She remains single as no one is considered noble sufficient for her, but when a mentor arrives they fall in love.
